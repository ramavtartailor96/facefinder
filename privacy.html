Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

FaceFinder is a Windows desktop application that allows users to search their own Google Drive photos using facial recognition technology.

Information We Access

When you sign in using Google OAuth, FaceFinder requests permission to access your Google Drive only after your explicit authorization.

How Your Data Is Used

  • Images are downloaded directly from your Google Drive to your own computer.
  • Face embeddings are generated locally on your device.
  • The searchable face index is stored locally on your computer.

Local Processing

All facial recognition processing occurs locally on your device. No images, embeddings, or search indexes are uploaded to any developer-controlled server.

Data Sharing

FaceFinder does not sell, share, or transmit your personal data or Google Drive files to any third party.

Google User Data

FaceFinder only accesses Google Drive files that you explicitly authorize. The application does not modify or delete your Google Drive content.

Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the project's GitHub repository.

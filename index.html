About FaceFinder

FaceFinder is a Windows desktop application that helps users search their own Google Drive photo library using facial recognition technology.

The application downloads your photos directly from your Google Drive, creates facial embeddings locally on your computer, and allows fast face-based searching without uploading your images to any external server.

Features

  • 🔍 Search thousands of photos by face
  • ☁️ Works with your Google Drive account
  • 💻 100% local AI processing
  • 🔒 No image uploads to developer servers
  • ⚡ Fast local face indexing and searching
  • 🖥️ Simple Windows desktop application

Privacy First

Your privacy is a core design principle.

  • Images are downloaded directly to your own computer.
  • Face recognition is performed entirely on your device.
  • Face embeddings and search indexes remain on your computer.
  • No personal photos are uploaded to developer-controlled servers.
  • The application only accesses your Google Drive after your explicit authorization.

Google Drive Access

FaceFinder requests access to Google Drive solely to download the images that you authorize for local processing. The application does not modify, delete, or share your Google Drive files.
